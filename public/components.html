Anatomy noun glossary

Every component composes from a stable noun set defined in §7.0. New variants reuse existing nouns. New nouns require G4 approval. The glossary is the cross-product translator: a "centre-value" on a RALIA risk gauge means the same as a "centre-value" on a RisqBase operations gauge.
