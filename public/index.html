Four signature elements

Every RisqBase surface is recognisably built on these four primitives. They appear together in the marketing site, RALIA, Cortex, and every component on this site.

IRIS

IRIS teal

The narrative-AI surface colour. Used for IRIS chat, AI-driven insights, and Gauge default arc.

Gauge family

Severity-encoded arc + numeral. Anchors any "how-much" view across the platform.

📄 GDPR Art. 35

Citation chip

Verified-source attribution. Every AI-asserted claim that touches user data carries one of these.

Concentric arc pattern

Background motif on hero sections; informs Gauge geometry, radial chart families, and Iris status rings.
